# Comparisons

How Stowage compares to projects that overlap with it.

The pages here are **explanation**, not **reference** — they exist
to help you decide whether Stowage is the right tool, not to
exhaustively document the alternatives.

For every claim about a non-Stowage product, the responsible thing
to do is verify against that product's own docs. The pages below
flag any claim that hasn't been verified against a primary source.
